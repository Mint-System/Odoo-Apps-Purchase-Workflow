=========================
Purchase Partner Incoterm
=========================

This module adds a field on the Sales & Purchases tab of the partner form where you can register the default incoterms for new purchase orders for this customer.

  :height: 100
  :width: 100
  :alt: Icon

Configuration
~~~~~~~~~~~~~
